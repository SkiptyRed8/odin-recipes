Egg Recipe

Learn how to make the perfect sunny-side-up eggs with crispy edges and a runny yolk!

This simple yet delicious recipe will help you achieve a beautifully cooked egg every time

perfect for breakfast or as a topping for your favorite dishes.

Ingridients

  • 500g pork (shoulder or belly), diced
  • 200g pork liver, diced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional, for richer flavor)
  • 1 cup water or broth
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • ¼ cup green peas (optional)
  • ¼ cup raisins (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (patis) or to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

How to Cook

  1. Heat the Pan – Place a non-stick or well-greased pan over low to medium heat.
  2. Add oil or Butter - Pour about 1 tablespoon of cooking oil or melt a small amount of butter in the pan
  3. Crack the egg -gently crack an egg into a small bowl, then carefully slide it into the pan to keep the yolk intact.
  4. Cook -gently let the egg cook undistributed for about 2-3 minutes until the whites are fully set, but the yolk remains runny.
  5. Cover the pn - (optional) for a slightly firmer yolk, cover the pan wth a lid for a few seconds to let the stream cook the top.
  6. Season to taste - Sprinklewith salt and pepper as desired.
  7. Serve and enjoy - Carefullu slide the egg onto a plate and serve hot with toast, rice, or your favoritebreakfast sides!
